About

Shark Tank is an entrepreneurial competition among Wheaton students modeled after the eponymous television show. Students with viable business ideas will present to a panel of judges and a few will be selected to receive mentoring. Next spring, there will be a second tank where those student entrepreneurs will be in front of true "sharks": investors able to grow their businesses but with a critical eye for success. The point is to have students across disciplines engage with business ventures and entrepreneurship.
