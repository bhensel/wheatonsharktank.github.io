About

Presented by the Student Alumni Board and Opus, Shark Tank is an entrepreneurial competition among Wheaton College students modeled after the eponymous television show. Students from all experience levels and backgrounds with viable business ideas present to a panel of judges; a set of winning groups will be propelled on a path of mentorship and growth. Last fall 13 groups competed to make it to the next round - now only 5 remain. This spring, these groups will present at a second tank, where the student entrepreneurs will be in front of true "sharks": investors able to grow their businesses with a critical eye for success. Our mission is to help students across disciplines engage in addressing the world's brokenness through innovation and entrepreneurship.

Shark Tank 2015

March 17th, 2015
Barrows Auditorium

Thursday night, five teams from all classes and majors will present their businesses and progress since fall Shark Tank to an audience and panel of judges at Wheaton College. Be there to support your friends as they compete for thousands of dollars in prize money and show off their work to investors in the audience. As the culmination of countless hours of work from the student entrepreneurs, it promises to be a night to remember.
